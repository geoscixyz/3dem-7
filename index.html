The 3DEM symposium is organized by the Hohmann-Wannamaker Trust.

The Hohmann-Wannamaker Trust (HWT) was established in December 2022 as the continuation of the Gerald W. Hohmann Trust for Teaching and Research in Electromagnetic Geophysics, following the tragic death of trustee Phil Wannamaker in August 2022.

The Hohmann-Wannamaker Trust also laments the loss of another founding trustee as a result of the untimely death of Louise Pellerin in March 2023.

3DEM-7 will be dedicated to honoring Phil’s and Louise’s legacies in the worldwide EM community as scientists, educators, colleagues, and friends.
